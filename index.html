I am a fifth-year PhD. student in Mathematics at USTC, currently working under the supervision of Professor Xi Zhang. I earned my B.S. from USTC in June 2020.

My research mainly lies in complex analytic and differential geometry. Recently, I am interested in using the analytic tools to understanding the geometry of K\"ahler spaces under various analytic positivity.

[2] On the structure of compact K\"ahler manifolds with nonnegative holomorphic sectional curvature, (with X. Zhang), arXiv:2311.18779v4.

The main result is the establishment of a "pseudoeffective" version of Bochner-type theorem, which leads to a splitting of the tangent bundle. This is a crucial step towards structure theorems of the nonnegative holomorphic sectional curvature obtained by Professor S. Matsumura recently.
