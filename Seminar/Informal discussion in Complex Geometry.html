Informal discussion in Complex Geometry

This is a page recording the informal and private discussion centered around complex analysis and differential geometry, where we share our discoveries and insights. The goal is to develop and shape our interests and ideas in this field, with a particular focus on the analytic aspects of holomorphic vector bundles.

Schedule

Miyaoka-type inequality on minimal K\"ahler maifolds (张世宇) 2025/3/22
Abstract: At the the first part, I give a proof for Miyaoka-type inequality for minimal K\"ahler manifolds based on the recent results by Cao, Ou and Paun. This is related to my recent work. At the second part, I give a proof on comparing the semi-stability of two torsion-free sheaves on the resolution of singularities.

Bibiography:

  1. Bibiography:
    1. W. Ou, A characterization of uniruled compact Kähler manifolds.
    2. J. Cao and M. Paun, Remarks on Relative Canonical Bundles and Algebraicity Criteria for Foliations in Kähler context.
  2. Y. Miyaoka, The Chern Classes and Kodaira Dimension of a Minimal Variety
An introduction to parabolic sheaves. (蒋天枢) 2025/3/8
Abstract: I will give a brief introduction to the category of parabolic sheaves. Once you were familiar with the basic concepts. I will share my ideas of proving the Bogomolov-Gieseker inequality in the parabolic setting.

Bibiography:
  1. M. Maruyama and K. Yokogawa, Moduli of parabolic stable sheaves
  2. T. Mochizuki, Kobayashi-Hitchin correspondence for tame harmonic bundles and an application
The existence of Harder-Narasimhan filtration and the stability under a neighborhood of the nef class with rational coefficients. (张世宇) 2025/1/18
Abstract: The proof of Kobayashi's result in his book (p. 161) is incomplete. [Bruasse, Harder-Narasimhan filtration on non-Kähler manifolds] addressed the gap by considering saturated subsheaves as weakly holomorphic maps. I will provide a relatively standard proof based on the more algebraic method introduced in [GKPT14, Reflexive differential forms on singular spaces, Appendix], and explain the statement regarding the stability of HN-filtration in a neighorhood of a nef cohomology class with rational coefficients, based on [Cao13, https://arxiv.org/abs/1305.4397]. Eventually, I could prove the existence of (w_1...w_{n-1})-Harder-Narasimhan filtration of torsion free sheaves on normal varities, where w_1,...w_{n-1} are nef classes, estabilish the concept of (w_1,...,w_{n-1})-HN-positivty, and prove a related vanishing theorem on normal varities with rational singularities.
