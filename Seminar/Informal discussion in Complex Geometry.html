• The existence of Harder-Narasimhan filtration and the stability under a neighborhood of the nef class with rational coefficients (张世宇)

The proof of Kobayashi's result in the book (p. 161) is incomplete, with a gap addressed in [Bruasse, Harder-Narasimhan filtration on non-Kähler manifolds], where the approach of considering saturated subsheaves as weakly holomorphic maps is adapted. I will provide an alternative proof based on the more algebraic method introduced in [GKPT14, Reflexive differential forms on singular spaces, Appendix], and explain the statement that the stability of HN-filtration in a neighorhood of a nef cohomology class with rational coefficients based on [Cao13, https://arxiv.org/abs/1305.4397]. Eventually, I could prove that the existence of (w_1...w_{n-1})-Harder-Narasimhan filtration of torsion free sheaves on normal varities, where w_1,...w_{n-1} are nef classes, estabilish the concept of (w_1,...,w_{n-1})-HN-positivty, and prove a vanishing theorem on normal varities with rational singularities.
